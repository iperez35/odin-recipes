Selly's Bulalo

What is Bulalo?

Bulalo is a beef dish from the Philippines. It is a light colored soup that is made by cooking beef shanks and bone marrow until the collagen and fat has been melted into the clear broth.

It typically includes leafy vegetables, corn on the cob, scallions, onions, garlic, ginger, and fish sauce. Potatoes, carrots, or taro can also be added. Bulalo is commonly eaten with rice.

Ingredients For Making Simple Bulalo

  • 2lbs Beef Shank
  • 1/2 Cabbage
  • 1 Bundle Bok Choy
  • 2 Cobs Corn
  • 2 Tablespoons Whole Peppercorn
  • 1/2 Cup Green Onions
  • 1 Red Onion
  • 34 Ounces Water
  • Fish Sauce To Taste

Steps

  1. In a big cooking pot, pour in the water and bring to a boil.
  2. Add the beef shank followed by the onion and whole peppercorn and simmer for 1.5 hours or until meat is tender.
  3. Add the corn and simmer for another 10 minutes
  4. Add the fish sauce, cabbage, and green onion
